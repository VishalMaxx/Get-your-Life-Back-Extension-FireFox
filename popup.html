Before you sign in

Linking an account syncs a few things to your own record in Google Firebase so they carry across your devices:

  • Your account email
  • The list of websites you've chosen to block
  • Focus/intervention events — the blocked domain, a timestamp, and whether you turned back or continued (never page content or full browsing history)
  • Your protocol/engagement state and the scores computed from it

We do not sell your data. It's never used for advertising, and never shared with third parties beyond Google Firebase acting as our storage/auth processor. Read the full Privacy Policy.
